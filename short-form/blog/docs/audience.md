# About the Target Audience

## Who is this blog for?

This blog (Typey Typey!) targets a technically literate, intellectually curious audience.
People who are engineers/developers by trade, but have a wider interest in
topics such as natural sciences, psychology, linguistics, and more.

Readers are expected to have **no background knowledge** for any non-technical
article, and aren't expected to have studied a topic in the humanities since
GCSEs.

## Where could this blog be found?

Blogs with the same sort of target audience are

- [HackerNews](https://news.ycombinator.com/)
- [Gwern](https://gwern.net/)
- [Astral Codex Ten](https://www.astralcodexten.com/)

## Features the Target Audience Expect

- Technical hook needed
- References/links
- Images, diagrams
- Informal tone
